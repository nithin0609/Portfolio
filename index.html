My Journey ‚ñº

Born in Bangalore - The Tech Capital

Started my life in India's Silicon Valley, where innovation is in the air. Growing up surrounded by technology probably sparked my interest in data and analytics without me even realizing it.

Age 16 - Off to Mangalore

Moved to Mangalore for high school and pre-university. This was my first experience living away from home, learning independence, and discovering coastal Karnataka's amazing food scene (those Mangalorean buns though!).

Back to Bangalore - University Years

Returned to Bangalore for my bachelor's degree. But here's where life got interesting - at just 18, I started supporting my dad's construction business. While other students were just studying, I was managing projects, handling teams, dealing with vendors, and learning what it really means to run a business.

What I learned from construction: Project management, team dynamics, budget handling, client relationships, and how to stay calm when everything seems to be going wrong (which happens a lot in construction!).

The Big Move - Hello Australia!

After years in construction and business, I realized I wanted to upskill, meet new people, explore different cultures, and honestly - live that abroad dream we all have. So I packed my bags and headed to Adelaide.

Australia has been an adventure. From figuring out public transport to understanding Aussie slang (still not sure what "yeah nah" really means), it's been a journey of constant learning and growth.

Master of Data Science - University of Adelaide

Now pursuing my Master's in Data Science, combining my business experience with cutting-edge analytics. The goal? To be someone who doesn't just crunch numbers, but tells stories with data and drives real business impact.

I love cooking! My specialties are pizzas (yes, homemade dough and all), pasta with various sauces, and of course, authentic Indian food that reminds me of home. There's something therapeutic about cooking - it's like data science, but you can eat the results.

On the Road

Adelaide has amazing drives, and I'm always out exploring. Whether it's a coastal drive, heading to the hills, or just a long drive to clear my head - I love being behind the wheel. It's my moving meditation.

On the Field

Cricket: I'm a batsman - there's nothing quite like timing that perfect cover drive. Also play badminton to stay active and competitive. Sports taught me teamwork, strategy, and how to handle pressure.
